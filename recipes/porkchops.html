Pork Chops

    Nutrition Facts

  • Per Serving: 260 calories; protein 40.8g; carbohydrates 0.6g; fat 9.1g; cholesterol 106.6mg; sodium 148.2mg.

Ingredients

  • aluminum foil
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • cooking spray
  • 4 boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick

Directions

  1. step 1: Place pork chops on a plate and lightly spray both sides with cooking spray. Sprinkle both sides with ranch seasoning mix and let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  2. step 2: Spray the basket of an air fryer with cooking spray and preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. step 3: Place chops in the preheated air fryer, working in batches if necessary, to ensure fryer is not overcrowded.
  4. step 4: Cook for 5 minutes. Flip chops and cook 5 minutes more. Let rest on a foil-covered plate for 5 minutes before serving.
